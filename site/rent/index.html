🪪 Driving License Guide

Can You Drive in Japan?

Check before you depart

🇦🇺 🇺🇸 🇬🇧 🇨🇦 🇳🇿

Geneva Convention Countries

International Driving Permit (IDP) required. Get it from your local automobile association before traveling.

  • 🇦🇺 Australia, 🇺🇸 USA, 🇬🇧 UK, 🇨🇦 Canada, 🇳🇿 New Zealand, etc.
  • IDP valid for 1 year from issue date
  • Must carry both IDP and original license
🇹🇼 🇨🇭 🇩🇪 🇫🇷 🇧🇪 🇮🇹

JAF Translation Required

Official Japanese translation of your license is required instead of an IDP.

  • 🇹🇼 Taiwan, 🇨🇭 Switzerland, 🇩🇪 Germany, 🇫🇷 France, 🇧🇪 Belgium, 🇮🇹 Italy, etc.
  • Translation available at JAF offices in Japan
  • We can help arrange this — just ask!
📋 Apply for JAF Translation Online →
🇸🇬

Singapore Travellers

Great news! Singapore is a Geneva Convention country. Get your IDP before you fly — it's easy and affordable.

  • ✅ IDP from AA Singapore (~SGD 20, same-day)
  • ✈️ Direct flights: Changi ↔ Fukuoka (3.5 hours)
  • 🚐 Pick up your van 10 min from Fukuoka Airport
  • 💰 From ~SGD 150/day all-inclusive
✈️

Israeli Travellers

Israel is a Geneva Convention country. Get your International Driving Permit before you fly — it's quick and easy.

  • ✅ IDP from MEMSI or any post office (~₪80)
  • ✈️ Flights: TLV → Fukuoka via Seoul/Bangkok (~8-12h)
  • 🚐 Pick up your van 10 min from Fukuoka Airport
  • 💰 From ~$110 USD/day all-inclusive
🗺️ Suggested Route

Your 7-Day Kyushu Road Trip

A recommended route from Fukuoka — fully customizable to your interests

Day 1–2
Fukuoka → Aso
Drive through the countryside to the world’s largest volcanic caldera. Wake up to misty grasslands and wild horses at Kusasenri.
🌋 Daikanbo Viewpoint 🌿 Kusasenri Grassland ♨️ Kurokawa Onsen
Day 3–4
Aso → Beppu
Head to Japan’s onsen capital. Soak in steaming hot springs, explore the “Hells of Beppu,” and eat local jigoku-mushi (steam-cooked) cuisine.
♨️ Hyotan Onsen 🔥 Jigoku Meguri 🍜 Jigoku-mushi
Day 5
Beppu → Takachiho
Discover the mythical gorge where Japanese gods are said to have descended. Rent a boat and drift beneath the 17m Manai Falls.
⛩️ Takachiho Gorge ⚩️ Amano Iwato Shrine 🎭 Yokagura Dance
Day 6
Takachiho → Itoshima
Wind through mountain roads back to the coast. End the day watching the sunset at Sakurai Futamigaura with its iconic torii gate and sea rocks.
🌅 Futamigaura Sunset 🌴 Beach Cafes 🍵 Local Crafts
Day 7
Itoshima → Fukuoka
A short 40-minute drive back to base. Drop off the van, grab a bowl of Hakata tonkotsu ramen, and catch your flight home.
🍜 Hakata Ramen ✈️ 10 min to Airport
