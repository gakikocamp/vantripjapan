Pocket Loft

Our ultra-compact roof tent explorer, built on the highly safe and reliable Daihatsu Hijet Truck base. Sleeps up to 4 guests (2 in the roof tent, 2 inside the cabin) with direct indoor passage up to the tent. Perfect for couples seeking extreme driving ease, high fuel economy, and 360° views on Japan's narrow roads.

🏝️ ISLAND FERRY FRIENDLY
Popular with Yakushima & Goto Islands travellers! Shortest vehicle = cheapest ferry fare + easiest to book on the ferry. Highly recommended for island hopping.
