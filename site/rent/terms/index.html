Rental Terms & Conditions

Last updated: April 2026

Thank you for choosing VanTrip Japan. These terms apply to all campervan rentals operated by キャンプ女子株式会社 (Camp Joshi Inc.). Please read them carefully before booking.

1. Rental Period & Eligibility

  • Minimum rental period: 2 days
  • Drivers must be at least 21 years old with a valid driving license recognized in Japan
  • Accepted licenses: International Driving Permit (Geneva Convention), or JAF-translated license (Taiwan, Switzerland, Germany, France, Belgium, Italy, etc.)
  • Pick-up and return is at our Hakozaki Base (10 min from Fukuoka Airport)

2. Pricing & What's Included

All daily rental rates include:

  • ✅ Vehicle insurance (CDW — Collision Damage Waiver)
  • ✅ Basic camping gear set
  • ✅ English navigation system
  • ✅ Portable power station
  • ✅ 24/7 roadside assistance
  • ✅ Multilingual support (English, German, French, Chinese)
  • ✅ ETC card (electronic highway toll — pre-registration required)
🛡️ Insurance is included in the daily rate. You do NOT need to pay extra for basic insurance coverage (CDW).

3. Security Deposit

💳 Security Deposit: ¥50,000

A refundable security deposit of ¥50,000 is collected at pick-up. This deposit covers:

  • Any ETC (highway toll) charges incurred during your rental
  • Any fuel shortfall if the vehicle is not returned with a full tank
  • Any minor damages not covered by insurance

The deposit (minus any applicable charges) is fully refunded within 7 business days after vehicle return.

4. Insurance & Accident Liability

4.1 Included Insurance (CDW)

All rentals include Collision Damage Waiver (CDW) insurance as part of the daily rate. This covers:

  • Third-party liability (対人・対物)
  • Vehicle damage (車両損害)

4.2 Deductible (免責額 / Excess)

In the event of an accident, the renter is responsible for a deductible (excess) amount:

Coverage Type Deductible Amount
Vehicle damage (車両免責) Up to ¥50,000
Third-party property damage (対物免責) Up to ¥50,000

You may optionally purchase a CDW Deductible Waiver to reduce or eliminate the deductible. Ask us for details at booking.

4.3 Non-Operation Charge (NOC / 休業補償)

⚠️ Non-Operation Charge (NOC)

If the vehicle is damaged and cannot be rented to the next customer, a Non-Operation Charge will apply:

Condition NOC Amount
Vehicle is drivable (self-return possible) ¥20,000
Vehicle is NOT drivable (towing required) ¥50,000

The NOC applies regardless of fault and is separate from the insurance deductible.

4.4 Not Covered by Insurance

Insurance does NOT cover:

  • Damage caused while driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs
  • Damage caused by unauthorized drivers
  • Damage to tires, wheels, undercarriage from off-road or unpaved driving
  • Interior damage, loss of personal belongings
  • Violations of traffic laws (fines are the renter's responsibility)

5. Fuel Policy

The vehicle is provided with a full tank of fuel. Please return the vehicle with a full tank. If the fuel level is below full, refueling charges will be deducted from the security deposit at market rate + a ¥3,000 refueling service fee.

6. Cancellation Policy

Cancellation Timing Fee
7+ days before rental start Free cancellation
3–6 days before 30% of rental fee
1–2 days before 50% of rental fee
Day of rental / No-show 100% of rental fee

7. One-Way Drop-Off

One-way trips (pick up in Fukuoka, drop off in Tokyo or Osaka) are available with a surcharge. Please contact us for pricing.

8. Prohibited Use

  • Off-road driving or beach driving
  • Smoking inside the vehicle (¥50,000 cleaning fee)
  • Driving outside of Japan
  • Sub-leasing the vehicle
  • Towing other vehicles
