Kyushu Road Trip Planner

Design your perfect campervan adventure across Kyushu, Japan. Answer 3 quick questions and receive a curated day-by-day itinerary, campsites map, and van recommendation instantly.

1 How many days do you have?
🎒 3 to 5 Days Short road trip. Ideal for quick loops covering highlights like Mount Aso and Beppu hot springs.
🚐 6 to 8 Days The Classic Route. Spend ample time hiking volcanoes, gorge rowing, and coastal driving.
🗺️ 9+ Days Grand Kyushu Explorer. Covers the entire island including Nagasaki, Kagoshima, and beyond.
2 What do you want to experience?
♨️ Onsens & Volcanoes Relax in traditional hot spring villages (Beppu, Kurokawa) and explore volcanic peaks.
🌋 Nature & Adventure Row boats in Takachiho Gorge, hike the Kuju trails, and drive scenic mountain passes.
🌊 Coastal Roads & Surf Cruise the Nichinan coastline, visit seaside shrines, and explore sandy beaches.
🍣 Gourmet & Local Culture Eat tonkotsu ramen in Fukuoka, local beef in Kumamoto, and visit samurai castles.
3 Who is traveling?
👩‍❤️‍👨 Couple / Duo Perfect for pairs or friends. Compact and easy-to-drive campervans.
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family with Kids Need spacious beds, storage space, and multiple seating options.
👤 Solo Traveler Compact vanlife. Easy parking and maximum freedom on narrow mountain tracks.
